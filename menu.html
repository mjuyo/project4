MENU

Discover the heart of genuine Chinese cuisine at CHAN, where our small, affordable menu showcases home-style dishes crafted with simplicity and authenticity. Join us for a delightful dining experience that blends traditional flavors with budget-friendly comfort.

ShengJianBao 生煎包

$12.00

Also known as "pan-fried buns", is a Shanghai-origin Chinese dumpling filled with minced pork and savory broth. Pan-fried for a crispy bottom and soft, steamed top, it offers a unique and flavorful experience.

KungPao Chicken 宫保鸡丁

$18.00

Tender diced chicken, crisp veggies, and roasted peanuts stir-fried to perfection, showcasing the authentic Sichuan blend of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors in every bite.

ZhaJiangMian 炸酱面

$18.00

Springy noodles adorned with a savory minced meat sauce, accentuated with aromatic spices. An authentic taste of Chinese food that promises a delightful fusion of textures and flavors.

LuRouFan 卤肉饭

$20.00

Succulent braised pork belly, bathed in a savory soy-based sauce with aromatic spices. A traditional Taiwanese dish that promises a blend of tender meat and flavorful rice, a taste of home in every bite.

YuXiang RouSi 鱼香肉丝

$18.00

Stir-fried tender pork strips with a variety of vegetables, enhanced by the captivating "fish fragrance" sauce. This dish is a dance of sweet, sour and spiciness, offering a true taste of Sichuan culinary.

Sausage Fried Rice 香肠炒饭

$16.00

Fragrant jasmine rice wok-tossed with succulent sausage slices. This dish is a celebration of the classic combination of seasoned rice and savory sausage, promising a flavorful experience in every bite.
